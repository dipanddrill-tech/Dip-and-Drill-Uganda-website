About Us

Holistic Water Services with a Strong Technical Team

Dip and Drill Uganda Limited is a Ugandan water solutions company committed to serving homeowners, institutions, businesses, and rural farmers with dependable and practical water systems.

We do not stop at drilling a borehole. We take a holistic approach by considering source investigation, drilling, casing, well development, pumping, storage, piping, irrigation, water testing, maintenance, and after-sales support.

This approach helps us serve both town and rural clients who need complete water systems for household use, commercial use, farming, and future expansion.
